Who We Are

Where Culture Meets the Contemporary.

SissyTrends was born not from a boardroom — but from a deep, unshakeable love for the saree. A love for the way it moves, the way it speaks, and the way it makes a woman feel absolutely, unapologetically herself.

We are a young boutique with big dreams and even bigger passion. Every piece we bring to you is chosen with intention — not just for its beauty, but for the story it carries and the confidence it gives. We believe sarees are not relics of the past; they are the boldest fashion statement of today.

At SissyTrends, we sit at the intersection of rich Indian textile heritage and the clean, confident sensibility of the modern woman. We curate sarees and jewellery that honour tradition while fully embracing the now — because that balance is exactly what makes Indian fashion so extraordinary.

Our Journey

How SissyTrends Came to Be

❧

  • THE SPARK

    A LOVE THAT STARTED YOUNG

    It started with a grandmother's silk saree, the weight of it, the shimmer, the way it transformed whoever wore it. That feeling never left. It grew into a conviction: every woman deserves to feel that way.

  • THE VISION

    CULTURE MEETS CONTEMPORARY

    We saw a gap — beautiful sarees that spoke only to tradition, or modern styles that had lost their soul entirely. SissyTrends was imagined to live right in between: cultural richness, modern confidence, no compromise.

  • THE BEGINNING

    THE BOUTIQUE IS BORN

    SissyTrends opened its doors in Coimbatore — a small boutique with a clear identity and an enormous amount of heart. Our first collection sold out before the week was over. We knew we were onto something real.

  • TODAY

    GROWING WITH GRATITUDE

    We're now reaching women all across India through our digital boutique — with a growing collection of sarees, imitation jewellery, and styled looks curated with the same passion that started it all. This is just the beginning.
